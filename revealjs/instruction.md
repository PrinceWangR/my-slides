# slide切换效果：

"default", "fade", "slide", "convex", "concave", "zoom" or "none".

## 用法：
1. {data-transition="zoom"} 
2. {data-transition="zoom-in fade-out"}
3. {data-transition-speed="slow"/"fast"}
